Who should compete
Clover math competitions are open to all students in 8th grade and below.
The 2 Forms
Because of a large interest by elementary school students in the competition, we have created a form A and a form B for the test. A is suited towards middle schoolers while B is tailored more for elementary students. Both tests are in the same format but have different difficulty levels. Only students in 5th grade and under are allowed to compete in the B test. However, both middle schoolers and elementary students up for the challenge may compete in the A test. Note that prizes are different for each form: refer to our home page for more info. To decide what form more suited for a student, we recommend the student to look over these questions to help decide what form they are more suited for.
Test format
  • 30 Questions.
  • 60 Minutes.
  • NO Calculator.
  • All questions are free-response, with a space to write your final answer. Only your final answer will be scored.
  • The questions are in increasing difficulty.
  • Scoring system TBD.
Why take our test?
  • A great opportunity to practice for the AMC and Mathcounts cycles!
  • There will be an optional review session right after the competition ends where the authors will go over all the problems.
