Who are we?
Clover is an organization of high schoolers with a love for math committed to inspiring the same passion among younger students. With our combined expertise, we seek to cultivate a community of mathematicians by organizing and providing engaging opportunities for exploration, learning, and of course, competition. If you'd like to learn more about our talented team of mathematicians, more info is below.
Locations
Clover is a Naperville, Illinois based organization.
